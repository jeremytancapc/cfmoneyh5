## Design Context

### Users
Singaporeans aged 21-55 applying for personal loans online. They are often stressed about their financial situation and need reassurance that they are in safe, competent hands. Many are on mobile. The moment of submitting a loan application is high-anxiety — they need calm clarity, not excitement.

### Brand Personality
Trustworthy, Fast, Approachable. CF Money is a licensed Singapore money lender (since 2011, 200,000+ customers). The brand should feel legitimate and human — regulated without being cold, efficient without being transactional.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light theme. Clean, professional, and warm. The existing palette — brand-blue `#0033AA` + brand-teal `#06DEC0` on a near-white neutral base — is the anchor. Quicksand headings bring warmth and approachability. Raleway body is readable and grounded. OKLCH tokens are already defined in globals.css. The grain texture on the page body adds analogue texture.

Anti-references: anything that looks like a fintech startup trying to feel "disruptive" (neon accents, dark mode, purple gradients). This is a licensed lender, not a crypto app. Every design decision should build trust.

### Design Principles
1. **Clarity over cleverness** — every screen has one clear job. Don't crowd it.
2. **Progressive disclosure** — reveal information at the pace the user can absorb it. The loading screen builds anticipation; the results page delivers with confidence.
3. **Confidence through transparency** — show the user exactly what they agreed to, what will happen next, and where to go. No surprises.
4. **Motion serves meaning** — animations signal progress and state change, not decoration. The staggered progress bar communicates that data is being processed, not that the developer wanted to add a cool animation.
